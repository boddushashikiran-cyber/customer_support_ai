TechMart Electronics — Warranty Policy

Standard Warranty
All TechMart-branded hardware products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions under normal use.

What's Covered
- Manufacturing defects
- Component failure not caused by external damage
- Battery capacity dropping below 70% within the warranty period (for battery-powered devices)

What's Not Covered
- Physical damage (drops, cracks, liquid damage)
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Normal wear and tear (e.g. cosmetic scratches)
- Software issues unrelated to hardware failure (see Technical Support instead)

Extended Warranty
An optional Extended Warranty Plan (+1 or +2 years) can be purchased within 30 days of the original purchase, extending the same coverage terms.

How to Claim Warranty
1. Contact support with your order ID and a description of the issue.
2. Our team may ask for photos/videos or request the device be shipped to our service center.
3. Approved claims result in a free repair, replacement part, or unit replacement, at TechMart's discretion, within 10-15 business days.

Installation & Setup Issues
Installation problems (e.g. app won't install, setup wizard errors) are handled by Technical Support and are not warranty claims.
