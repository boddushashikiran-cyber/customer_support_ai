TechMart Electronics — Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping (India)
- Standard delivery: 3-5 business days, free for orders above ₹999.
- Express delivery: 1-2 business days, flat ₹149 charge.
- Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation on business days.

International Shipping
- Delivery timelines range from 7-15 business days depending on destination country.
- Customs duties and import taxes, if applicable, are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in the order total.
- We currently ship to 40+ countries including the US, UK, UAE, Australia, and most of the EU.

Order Tracking
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also check order status under Account > Orders > Track Order.

Delayed or Lost Shipments
If your order has not arrived within the estimated delivery window, please contact support with your order ID. We will investigate with the courier partner and, if the shipment is confirmed lost, issue a replacement or full refund.

Address Changes
Shipping addresses can be changed only before the order is dispatched. Once dispatched, address changes are not possible; please contact support immediately if you suspect an error.
