TechMart Electronics — Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Physical Products
Refunds for physical products can be requested within 15 days of delivery, provided the item is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the invoice. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

2. Subscription Plans (Premium / Pro)
- Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime; you retain access until the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are issued for unused days within a cycle.
- Annual subscriptions are eligible for a pro-rated refund if cancelled within the first 30 days.
- If a payment was successful but the paid feature (e.g. Premium) has not unlocked within 30 minutes, this is treated as a sync error, not a refund case — contact Technical Support first; Billing will investigate in parallel if the issue persists.

3. Digital Products & Licenses
Digital licenses and downloadable software are non-refundable once the license key has been activated, except in cases of a verified defect.

4. How to Request a Refund
Submit a refund request through Account > Orders > Request Refund, or contact support@techmart-electronics.example with your order ID. Refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5-10 business days depending on your bank.

5. Refund Rejections
Refund requests may be rejected if: the return window has passed, the product shows signs of misuse or damage not covered under warranty, or the item is on our non-returnable list (e.g. opened earphones/earbuds for hygiene reasons).
