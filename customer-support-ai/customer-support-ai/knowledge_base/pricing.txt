TechMart Electronics — Pricing & Plans

TechMart Basic (Free)
- Access to standard product catalog and order tracking
- Community support only

TechMart Premium — ₹299/month or ₹2,999/year (save ~16%)
- Priority customer support (under 2-hour response time)
- Free express shipping on all orders
- Extended 2-year warranty on eligible products
- Early access to new product launches

TechMart Pro (for business customers) — ₹999/month or ₹9,999/year
- Everything in Premium
- Bulk order discounts (5-15% based on volume)
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority repair/replacement turnaround (5 business days)

Add-ons
- Extended Warranty Plan: ₹499 (+1 year) or ₹899 (+2 years)
- Accidental Damage Protection: ₹599/year

Notes
- All plans auto-renew unless cancelled before the renewal date.
- Upgrading from Premium to Pro mid-cycle is pro-rated automatically.
- Plan changes take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
